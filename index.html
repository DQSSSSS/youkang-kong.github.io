Youkang Kong

Chinese - 孔佑康

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Tsinghua University, affiliated with the Institute for Advanced Study, and advised by Prof. Heung-Yeung Shum. Before joining Tsinghua, I received my B.Eng. in Computer Science from the Honors School of Harbin Institute of Technology, Harbin Institute of Technology in 2021.

I have been a research intern at Microsoft Research Asia since 2020, where I work with Dr. Yang Liu, Dr. Yue Dong and Dr. Xin Tong on 3D-related research problems.

My primary research interests lie in computer graphics and 3D vision, with a focus on 3D generation, rendering, and modeling. I am particularly interested in building scalable and controllable methods for neural 3D content creation.
